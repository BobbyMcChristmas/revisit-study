# Training
Welcome to the study! Please read over this quick guide before starting the tasks.

**Verbalizing Your Thoughts**

This study includes **think-aloud protocols:**

+ **Speak aloud** any observations, confusions, or decisions that you make.
- You can **pause/resume audio recording** using the “Recording audio” button at the top-right of the screen
* Before proceeding to the next screen, make sure to check the box: **“Have you verbalized your thoughts?”**

**Navigation**

+ If you have verbalized your thoughts, click on the **checkbox**

- Click **Next** after completing each task to move to the next task
